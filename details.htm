Artificial Intelligence is reshaping the media landscape. From drafting news briefs to automating fact-checking, AI tools are changing how newsrooms operate. Many see this as a way to enhance productivity, while others raise concerns about bias and authenticity.

What Role Does AI Play in Newsrooms?

AI is assisting journalists with data analysis, summarization, and even real-time translation. Tools like ChatGPT, Grammarly, and automated video editors are becoming standard in modern media houses.

Risks and Ethical Concerns

Despite benefits, concerns about misinformation, deepfakes, and over-reliance on AI tools remain. Media organizations are now tasked with developing ethical frameworks for using AI responsibly.
